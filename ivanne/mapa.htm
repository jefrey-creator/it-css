I was LSS to this song lately. MAPA of SB19, this song is trending online and in others social media platforms. For me MAPA is one of the greatest song I’ve ever heard.

MaPa means map. But the writer of the song give other meaning to this. MaPa stands for Mama ( a Tagalog term for Mother) and Papa ( a Tagalog term for Father. So in short Ma- mother and Pa- father.

Photo Credits to : @SB19Official

This song is dedicated to all parents out there. The song is written and sang in Tagalog form. The Lyric Video of MAPA posted in SB19 Official YouTube channel has gained million views. The said Lyric Video has being reviewed by a lot of YouTubers, MAPA has a lot of good reviews from all over the world.

The song appreciate our beloved parents that’s why people got hooked in this song. Not all of musicians are writing a song about their parents, just a few of song writers and musicians do that things. In the song it all tells how our mother and father do just to make us happy. How our parents do everything to provide for us. The song also tells that our parents is the reason why we live in this world.

 

And it is also stated in the lyrics that as children we will work for them to give back their hard work. Because  they always think of us and forget to care about themselves , so it is the time for us to take care of them and serve them.

 

“ Kaya't wag mag-alala

Ipikit Ang iyong Mata, tahan na

Pahinga muna ako na Ang bahala

Labis pa sa Labis Ang iyong nagawa

Ma,Pa pahinga muna

Ako na.”

-         This is the chorus part of the song.

 

And it also says in the lyrics that they are Our  MaPa (Mata and Paa) Mata in English is Eyes and  Paa in English is Feet. Eyes in every time the world changes and feet every time We crawl. They are also the cause of our breathing. It also says that we should not waste the remaining time and we should  focused all our attention on the things that are important and matter most. And the most important part of the song is that no matter where we go, we will not get lost because we carry the MAP. And Wherever We Go We Know Where We Come From.

Photo Credits to : @SB19Official

SB19 is a Ppop boy group. They are Consisting of 5 members: Pablo, Stell, Josh, Ken and Justin. The said group are already dominating the world. Recently they released a Band Version of MAPA with the group Ben&Ben and it also received a lot of good and positive comments from the viewers.

 

Authors note: If you want to hear this song just visit the YouTube channel of SB19. And also you can follow them on their social media accounts. I assure you that this song is worth listening for.
